Refund policy

We're bummed things didn't work out for you, but here's the good news:

MALAYSIA HASSLE-FREE RETURNS

If your Black Market order has been delivered wrongly / any defects
on the items, you are entitle for a correct order free of charge,
by returning us the defect order. All of the delivery charges will
be bear by Black Market within Malaysia. Refund of cash or payment
is not available unless there is a website error (i.e. wrong pricing
showed on the website) refund of cash or payment to your original
payment method (i.e. Credit Card, Paypal) will require a handling &
processing fee, which will be deducted from the amount to be refunded to you.

If your Black Market order has been delivered wrongly / any defects on the
items, here are some of the alternatives for you to reach us regarding this issue:

STEP 1. Bring your defected Black Market to the nearest post office
and fill in this address in the consignment note:

Kampus Utama, Jalan Genting Kelang, 53300 Kuala Lumpur,
Wilayah Persekutuan Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia

STEP 2. Take a CLEAR photo of the receipt received from the post office and email it to ask@BlackMarketcom together with your Order ID, Defect Details, and Bank Account Number (preferably Maybank). The postage fees will be banked in to you on the Friday of that week.

STEP 3. We will take at least 3-5 working days to prepare and send your new Black Market order. In the mean time, please do patiently wait for your order to arrive.
